About Hidden Junk

I created Hidden Junk because I believe Soweto has incredible fashion talent that deserves to be seen and supported. We work directly with local clothing artists and designers to showcase their work every week. No big brands — just real, unique pieces made right here in our community.

Our mission is simple: celebrate creativity, support local makers, and make it easy for you to wear something special.
